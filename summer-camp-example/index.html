My Top 3 Monsters from Monster Hunter Rise!

By: Christopher Glinka

What is Monster Hunter Rise?

Monster Hunter Rise is a Nintendo Switch and PC videogame released on March 26, 2021. It is a grind heavy multiplayer adventure game where players can hunt a variety of huge terrifying monsters that each showcase a number of unique habitats, abilities, and characteristics. Players can either hunt in groups or by themselves with the sole purpose of obtaining materials to craft a wide variety of upgrades, including, but not limited to weapons and armours.
